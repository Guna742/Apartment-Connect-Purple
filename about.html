Our Story

About Apartment Connect

We believe every apartment deserves a smarter, more connected digital future. We built it.

Our Mission

Empowering Every Community to Thrive Digitally

Apartment Connect was founded with one bold belief: modern living shouldn't be fragmented across 10 different apps, notice boards, and disconnected systems. Residents deserve a unified, intelligent platform that respects their privacy and enhances their daily life.

Our mission is to digitally transform residential ecosystems by connecting residents, staff, managers, and vendors in one powerful, inclusive platform — making every community smarter, safer, and more connected.

Our Journey

Building the Future of Living

2022 — The Idea

Born from Necessity

A group of designers and property managers frustrated by legacy systems decided to build the modern residential operating system.

2023 — First Complex

Pilot Launch

Launched at 5 premium residential complexes in Bengaluru — 5,000 residents onboarded within weeks.

2024 — Scale Up

Series A & Expansion

Raised ₹12 Cr in Series A. Expanded to 60+ communities across 8 major cities.

2025 — Now

120+ Communities, 50K+ Users

The leading residential ecosystem platform in the region, redefining modern communal living.
